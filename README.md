# Cameroon Sales & Revenue Analysis
## Power BI Dashboard Project 🇨🇲📊

### Overview
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing 1,000 sales transactions 
across 6 Cameroonian cities for a trading company operating 
between 2023 and 2024.

### Tools Used
- **Python** (Pandas, NumPy) — data generation and preparation
- **Power BI Desktop** — interactive dashboard creation
- **Excel** — data storage and transfer

### Dashboard Features
- 📊 Total Revenue KPI card
- 📦 Total Quantity Sold KPI card
- 🔢 Total Transactions KPI card
- 🏙️ Revenue by City bar chart
- 📈 Monthly Revenue Trend line chart
- 🥧 Revenue by Category pie chart
- 🍩 Revenue by Channel donut chart
- 🔽 Interactive slicers for Year and City

### Key Insights
- Total revenue analyzed across 2023-2024
- 6 cities: Douala, Yaoundé, Bafoussam, Garoua, Bamenda, Maroua
- 5 product categories: Électronique, Alimentation, Vêtements, Mobilier, Cosmétiques
- 3 sales channels: Boutique, En ligne, Grossiste
